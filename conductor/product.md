# Initial Concept
A comprehensive set of hands-on labs for the Cisco CCNP ENCOR (350-401) exam.

# Product Definition - CCNP ENCOR Lab Series

## Target Audience
- **CCNP ENCOR (350-401) Exam Candidates:** Individuals seeking structured, hands-on practice to master exam blueprint topics.
- **Experienced Network Engineers:** Professionals looking for a reliable environment to refresh their knowledge of core routing protocols (EIGRP, OSPF, BGP) and services.

## Core Goals
- **GNS3 Apple Silicon Optimization:** Ensure all topologies and device platforms (c3725, c7200) are fully compatible and performant on Apple M1/M2/M3 hardware.
- **DeepSeek Standard Workbooks:** Deliver standardized, high-quality lab guides that include detailed objectives, step-by-step configuration, verification procedures, and full solutions.

## Key Features
- **Verification Cheatsheets:** Provide concise "cheat sheets" for every lab to help users quickly validate their configurations using industry-standard `show` and `debug` commands.
- **Troubleshooting Scenarios:** Integrate specific troubleshooting "tickets" within each chapter to simulate real-world issues and exam-style "find the fault" challenges.

## Scope of Coverage
- **Core Routing & Switching:** In-depth coverage of EIGRP, OSPF, BGP, STP, and EtherChannel configuration and optimization.
- **IP Services & Security:** Comprehensive labs for NAT, FHRP, AAA, ACLs, and VPN technologies.

## Learning Path
- **Topic-Focused Progression:** Labs are designed as standalone modules for specific exam blueprint bullets, ranging from introductory concepts to advanced optimization, allowing users to target specific weak areas.
